At [OpenAgents.com](https://openagents.com) you can chat with:
- Leading **base models** from Meta, Mistral, Anthropic, OpenAI, Perplexity, Cohere, and more
- Custom **agents** created by users that combine custom instructions, extra knowledge, and advanced plugins to provide unique functionality

## Chat with LLMs

Use the model dropdown at the top-left of the screen to select between models.

You can switch between models anytime, even in the middle of a chat.

Top-tier models require a Pro subscription ($10/month), including 100 responses per day.

## Chat with agents

Explore agents in the [Agent Store](https://openagents.com/store). Click on any agent to start a chat.

Agents are created by users. Quality may vary!
